About ME

I grew up in Kyiv, Ukraine, and now placed in Drammen, Norway.

Since childhood, I`ve been drawn to creativity—always eager to express my unique vision. I started drawing at a very young age, knitting, and exploring all kinds of ways to bring ideas to life. At the same time, I developed a love for mathematics, enjoying the logic and problem-solving it offers. Over the years, this combination of creativity and analytical thinking evolved into a passion for design and web development. Alongside my creative pursuits, I`m also passionate about sports, which help me stay active, disciplined, and inspired.

Today, I`m focused on creating distinctive and memorable projects—whether it`s a sleek website or a design that stands out. I love building things that not only work but leave a lasting impression
